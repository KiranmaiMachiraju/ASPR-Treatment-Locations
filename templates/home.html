Vaccination & Medication Guidance

Flu Vaccine

Recommended annually for everyone over 6 months of age. High-risk groups include seniors, pregnant individuals, and those with chronic illnesses.

COVID-19 Vaccine

Recommended for everyone 6 months and older. Booster shots advised for adults, especially those at higher risk of severe illness.

Paxlovid / Antivirals

Antiviral treatments for COVID-19 for high-risk patients. Must be taken soon after symptom onset.

Lagevrio

Another antiviral option for COVID-19. Recommended for high-risk adults. Early administration is critical.

Veklury (Remdesivir)

Intravenous antiviral for hospitalized COVID-19 patients. Administered under medical supervision.

Other Antivirals

Includes Oseltamivir, Zanamivir, Peramivir, Baloxavir. Typically used for influenza and should follow healthcare provider guidance.
